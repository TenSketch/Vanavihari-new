Awards

  • Eco-Friendly Tourism Achievement:

    In 2010, the Chairperson and Vice Chairperson of CBET were honored with the Eco-Friendly Tourism Award by the former esteemed Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh, recognizing their commitment to sustainable tourism practices.

  • National Recognition:

    At the Second National Level Convention on "Sustainable Action and Network through Community Leaders Programme" (SANCALP), hosted by the Indian Institute of Bio-Social Research and Development (IBRAD) in Calcutta in 2010-11, the CBET Coordinator was acknowledged with a certificate of appreciation.

  • International Recognition Efforts:

    CBET, Maredumilli, has submitted proposals to the prestigious PATA (Pacific Asia Travel Association) international award, endorsed by the Department of Tourism, Government of Andhra Pradesh, as a commendation of its outstanding contributions to the tourism sector.
