Tribal Community

Konda Reddi

Konda Reddis inhabit the banks on either side of the Godavari River in the hilly and forested regions of East and West Godavari, and Khammam districts of Andhra Pradesh. Their mother tongue is Telugu.

Valmiki

Valmikis living in the Agency tracts of Andhra Pradesh are the only group recognized as Scheduled Tribes, primarily found in the agency areas of Visakhapatnam and East Godavari districts. Claiming descent from the sage Valmiki, the author of the Ramayana, the Valmiki tribe is divided into various 'gotrams' to regulate marriage institutions in Visakhapatnam tribal areas.
